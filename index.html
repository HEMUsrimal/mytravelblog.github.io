About Me

I am a final year Software Engineering student at the University of Sri Jayewardenepura, Faculty of Computing.

The Engineer

Specializing in Mobile App Development (Flutter) and Natural Language Processing. I have hands-on experience in High-Performance Computing, having built computer clusters using virtual machines and MPI.

The Researcher

Currently conducting research on a Real-time Bus Arrival Prediction System for Sri Lanka, utilizing machine learning to improve public transport efficiency. My technical interests extend to Computer Vision and CNN architectures like VGG-19 and ResNet.

The Creative

Beyond the terminal, I operate Udayanga Srimal Photography, focusing on event and portrait photography. I am also an active member of IEEE and have contributed to organizing major university events like Sililara 2025 and FusionX 1.0.
