Welcome to the Enchanting World of Sri Lanka

I'm Udayanga, your guide through the picturesque wonders of my homeland, Mahiyanganaya. Nestled amidst verdant landscapes, Mahiyanganaya boasts a treasure trove of natural marvels. From the majestic Rathna Waterfall to hidden gems tucked away in lush forests, each destination unveils a story of its own.

Popular Destinations

Dunhinda Waterfall Badulla

Known for its smoky dew spray (Dun means mist), this 64-meter fall is created by the Badulu Oya.

Yala National Park Katharagama

Home to the highest leopard densities in the world, Yala hosts 215 bird species across diverse ecosystems.

Baron's Cap / Thoppigala Batticaloa

A historic peak located 40km northwest of Batticaloa, offering unique vistas of the eastern landscape.

Rathna Ella Waterfall Hasalaka

The 14th tallest waterfall in Sri Lanka (101m), springing from the Knuckles mountain range.

Pasikuda Batticaloa

A world-renowned coastal resort town famous for its shallow turquoise waters and coral reefs.
